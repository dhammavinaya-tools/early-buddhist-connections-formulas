# SN48.1 Simple Version

At Sāvatthī. There the Blessed One addressed the bhikkhus thus:

“Bhikkhus, there are these five faculties. What five? The faculty of faith, the faculty of energy, the faculty of mindfulness, the faculty of samādhi, the faculty of wisdom. These are the five faculties.”
