# SN45.13 A Trainee

© Translated from the Pali by Bhikkhu Bodhi.  (More copyright information)
1At Sāvatthī. Then a certain bhikkhu approached the Blessed One … . Sitting to one side, that bhikkhu said to the Blessed One:

"Venerable sir, it is said, ‘a trainee, a trainee.’ In what way is one a trainee?"

2"Here, bhikkhu, one possesses a trainee's right view … a trainee's right samādhi. It is in this way that one is a trainee."
