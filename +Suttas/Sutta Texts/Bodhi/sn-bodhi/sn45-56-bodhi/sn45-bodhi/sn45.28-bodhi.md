# SN45.28 samādhi

At Sāvatthı̄. “Bhikkhus, I will teach you noble right samādhi with its supports and its accessories. Listen to that….

“And what, bhikkhus, is noble right samādhi with its supports and its accessories? There are: right view ... right mindfulness. The one-pointedness of mind equipped with these seven factors is called noble right samādhi ‘with its supports,’ and also ‘with its accessories.’”
