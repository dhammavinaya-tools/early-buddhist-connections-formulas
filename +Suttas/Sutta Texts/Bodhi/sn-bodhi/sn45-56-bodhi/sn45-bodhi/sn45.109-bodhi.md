# SN45.109-114 The Ocean

(In this version §§103–108 are identical with §§91–96, and §§109–114 with §§97–102, except for the following change:)

“Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu develops and cultivates right view … right samādhi, which has as its final goal the removal of lust, the removal of hatred, the removal of delusion.”
