# SN45.22 Unwholesome States

At Sāvatthı̄. “Bhikkhus, I will teach you unwholesome states and wholesome states. Listen to that….

“And what, bhikkhus, are unwholesome states? They are: wrong view ... wrong samādhi. These are called unwholesome states.

“And what, bhikkhus, are wholesome states? They are: right view ... right samādhi. These are called wholesome states.”
