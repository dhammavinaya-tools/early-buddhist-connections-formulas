# SN45.21 Wrongness

At Sāvatthı̄. “Bhikkhus, I will teach you wrongness and rightness. Listen to that…. \[18\]

“And what, bhikkhus, is wrongness? It is: wrong view ... wrong samādhi. This is called wrongness.

“And what, bhikkhus, is rightness? It is: right view ... right samādhi. This is called rightness.”
