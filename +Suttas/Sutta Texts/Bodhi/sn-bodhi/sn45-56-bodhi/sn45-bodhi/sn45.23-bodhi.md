# SN45.23 The Way (1)

At Sāvatthı̄. “Bhikkhus, I will teach you the wrong way and the right way. Listen to that….

“And what, bhikkhus, is the wrong way? It is: wrong view ... wrong samādhi. This is called the wrong way.

“And what, bhikkhus, is the right way? It is: right view ... right samādhi. This is called the right way.”
