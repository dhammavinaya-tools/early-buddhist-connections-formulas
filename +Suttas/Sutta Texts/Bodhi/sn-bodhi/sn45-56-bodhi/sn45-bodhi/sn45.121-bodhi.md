# SN45.121-126 The Ocean

(In this version §§115–120 are identical with §§91–96, and §§121–126 with §§97–102, except for the following change:)

“Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu develops and cultivates right view … right samādhi, which has the Deathless as its ground, the Deathless as its destination, the Deathless as its final goal.”
