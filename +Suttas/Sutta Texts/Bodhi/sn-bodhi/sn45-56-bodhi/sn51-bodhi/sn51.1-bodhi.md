# SN51.1 From the Near Shore

“Bhikkhus, these four bases for spiritual power, when developed and cultivated, lead to going beyond from the near shore to the far shore. What four? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu develops the basis for spiritual power that possesses samādhi due to desire and volitional formations of striving. He develops the basis for spiritual power that possesses samādhi due to energy and volitional formations of striving. He develops the basis for spiritual power that possesses samādhi due to mind and volitional formations of striving. He develops the basis for spiritual power that possesses samādhi due to investigation and volitional formations of striving. These four bases for spiritual power, when developed and cultivated, lead to going beyond from the near shore to the far shore.”
