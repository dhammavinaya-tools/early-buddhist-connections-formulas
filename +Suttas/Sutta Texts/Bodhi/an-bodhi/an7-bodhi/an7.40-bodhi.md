# AN7.40 Mastery (1)

> **Division:** IV. DEITIES

“Bhikkhus, possessing seven qualities, a bhikkhu exercises mastery over his mind and is not a servant of his mind. What seven? Here, (1) a bhikkhu is skilled in samādhi, (2) skilled in the attainment of samādhi, (3) skilled in the duration of samādhi, (4) skilled in emergence from samādhi, (5) skilled in fitness for samādhi, (6) skilled in the area of samādhi, and (7) skilled in resolution regarding samādhi. Possessing these seven qualities, a bhikkhu exercises mastery over his mind, and is not a servant of his mind.”
