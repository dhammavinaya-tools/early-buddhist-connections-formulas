# AN7.45 Accessories

> **Division:** V. THE GREAT SACRIFICE

“There are, bhikkhus, these seven accessories of samādhi. What seven? Right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, and right mindfulness. One-pointedness of mind equipped with these seven factors is called noble right samādhi ‘with its supports’ and ‘with its accessories.’” \[41\]
