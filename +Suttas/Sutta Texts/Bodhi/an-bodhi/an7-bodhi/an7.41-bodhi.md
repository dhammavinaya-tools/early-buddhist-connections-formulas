# AN7.41 Mastery (2)

> **Division:** IV. DEITIES

“Bhikkhus, possessing seven qualities, Sāriputta exercises mastery over his mind and is not a servant of his mind. What seven? Here, (1) Sāriputta is skilled in samādhi, (2) skilled in the attainment of samādhi, (3) skilled in the duration of samādhi, (4) skilled in emergence from samādhi, (5) skilled in fitness for samādhi, (6) skilled in the area of samādhi, and (7) skilled in resolution regarding samādhi. Possessing these seven qualities, Sāriputta exercises mastery over his mind and is not a servant of his mind.”
