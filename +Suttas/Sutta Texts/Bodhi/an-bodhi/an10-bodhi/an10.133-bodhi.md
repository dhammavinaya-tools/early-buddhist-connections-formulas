# AN10.133 Eleventh

> **Division:** III. PURIFIED

“Bhikkhus, there are these ten right courses. What ten? Right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samādhi, right knowledge, and right liberation. These are the ten right courses.”
