# AN10.112 One Beyond Training (2)

> **Division:** I. AN ASCETIC’S PERCEPTIONS

“Bhikkhus, there are these ten qualities of one beyond training. What ten? The right view of one beyond training; the right intention … the right speech … the right action … the right livelihood … the right effort … the right mindfulness … the right samādhi … the right knowledge … the right liberation of one beyond training. These are the ten qualities of one beyond training.”
