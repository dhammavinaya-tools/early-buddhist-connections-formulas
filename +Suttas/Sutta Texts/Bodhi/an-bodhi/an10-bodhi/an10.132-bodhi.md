# AN10.132 Tenth

> **Division:** III. PURIFIED

“Bhikkhus, there are these ten wrong courses. What ten? Wrong view, wrong intention, wrong speech, wrong action, wrong livelihood, wrong effort, wrong mindfulness, wrong samādhi, wrong knowledge, and wrong liberation. These are the ten wrong courses.”
