# AN10.51 down to:\] … But if, by such self-examination, a bhikkhu knows: ‘I am often without longing … and collected,’ then he should base himself on those same wholesome qualities and make a further effort to reach the destruction of the taints.”

> **Division:** I. ONE’S OWN MIND
