# AN10.122 Taints

> **Division:** II. PACCOROHANṆĪ

“Bhikkhus, these ten things, when developed and cultivated, lead to the destruction of the taints. What ten? Right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samādhi, right knowledge, and right liberation. These ten things, when developed and cultivated, lead to the destruction of the taints.”
