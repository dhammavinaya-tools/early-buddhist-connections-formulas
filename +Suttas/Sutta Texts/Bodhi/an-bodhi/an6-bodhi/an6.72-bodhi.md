# AN6.72 Strength

> **Division:** II. NON-RETURNER

“Bhikkhus, possessing six qualities a bhikkhu is incapable of attaining strength in samādhi. What six? (1) Here, a bhikkhu is not skilled in the attainment of samādhi; (2) he is not skilled in the duration of samādhi; (3) he is not skilled in emergence from samādhi; (4) he does not practice carefully; (5) he does not practice persistently; and (6) he does not do what is suitable. Possessing these six qualities, a bhikkhu is incapable of attaining strength in samādhi.

“Bhikkhus, possessing six qualities a bhikkhu is capable of attaining strength in samādhi. What six? \[428\] (1) Here, a bhikkhu is skilled in the attainment of samādhi; (2) he is skilled in the duration of samādhi; (3) he is skilled in emergence from samādhi; (4) he practices carefully; (5) he practices persistently; and (6) he does what is suitable. Possessing these six qualities, a bhikkhu is capable of attaining strength in samādhi.”
