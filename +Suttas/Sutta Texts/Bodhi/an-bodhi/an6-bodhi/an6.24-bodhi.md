# AN6.24 Himalayas

> **Division:** III. THE UNSURPASSED THINGS

“Bhikkhus, possessing six qualities, a bhikkhu might split the Himalayas, the king of mountains, how much more then vile ignorance! What six? Here, a bhikkhu is (1) skilled in the attainment of samādhi; (2) skilled in the duration of samādhi; (3) skilled in emergence from samādhi; (4) skilled in fitness for samādhi; (5) skilled in the area of samādhi; and (6) skilled in resolution regarding samādhi. \[312\] Possessing these six qualities, a bhikkhu might split the Himalayas, the king of mountains, how much more then vile ignorance!”
