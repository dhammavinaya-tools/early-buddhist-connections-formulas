# AN5.92 Accomplishments (2)

> **Division:** V. KAKUDHA

“Bhikkhus, there are these five accomplishments. What five? Accomplishment in virtuous behavior, accomplishment in samādhi, accomplishment in wisdom, accomplishment in liberation, and accomplishment in the knowledge and vision of liberation. These are the five accomplishments.”
