# AN5.113 samādhi

> **Division:** II. ANDHAKAVINDA

“Bhikkhus, possessing five qualities, a bhikkhu is incapable of entering and dwelling in right samādhi. What five? Here, a bhikkhu cannot patiently endure forms, sounds, odors, tastes, and tactile objects. Possessing these five qualities, a bhikkhu is incapable of entering and dwelling in right samādhi.

“Bhikkhus, possessing five \[other\] qualities, a bhikkhu is capable of entering and dwelling in right samādhi. What five? \[138\] Here, a bhikkhu can patiently endure forms, sounds, odors, tastes, and tactile objects. Possessing these five qualities, a bhikkhu is capable of entering and dwelling in right samādhi.”
