# AN2.73

> **Division:** VII. HAPPINESS

“Bhikkhus, there are these two kinds of happiness. What two? The happiness of samādhi and the happiness without samādhi. These are the two kinds of happiness. Of these two kinds of happiness, the happiness of samādhi is foremost.”
