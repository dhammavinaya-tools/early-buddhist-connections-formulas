# AN4.242 Benefits of a Good Person

> **Division:** IV. KAMMA

“Bhikkhus, by relying on a good person, four benefits are to be expected. What four? One grows in noble virtuous behavior; one grows in noble samādhi; one grows in noble wisdom; and one grows in noble liberation. By relying on a good person, these four benefits are to be expected.”
