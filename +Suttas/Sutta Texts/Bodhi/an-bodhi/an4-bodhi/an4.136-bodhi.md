# AN4.136 Virtuous Behavior (1)

> **Division:** IV. PERSONS

“Bhikkhus, there are these four kinds of persons found existing in the world. What four? (1) Here, bhikkhus, some person does not fulfill virtuous behavior, samādhi, and wisdom. (2) Another person fulfills virtuous behavior but does not fulfill samādhi and wisdom. (3) Still another person fulfills virtuous behavior and samādhi but does not fulfill wisdom. (4) And still another person fulfills virtuous behavior, samādhi, and wisdom. These are the four kinds of persons found existing in the world.”
