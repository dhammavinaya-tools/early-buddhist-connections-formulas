# AN4.150 Cores

> **Division:** V. SPLENDORS

“Bhikkhus, there are these four cores. What four? The core of virtuous behavior, the core of samādhi, the core of wisdom, and the core of liberation. These are the four cores.”

The Fourth Fifty
